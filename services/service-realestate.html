Comprehensive Legal Solutions in Real Estate

At Al-Arabia, we provide integrated legal services in real estate according to Syrian law, ensuring the protection of your property rights and facilitating your transactions.

Our real estate law services include:

  • Review of real estate contracts (sale - purchase - lease - exchange)
  • Property registration procedures in the real estate registry
  • Resolution of real estate disputes and possession/expropriation cases
  • Property division and distribution procedures
  • Compensation cases for public utility expropriation
  • Completion of foreign ownership procedures for properties in Syria
  • Legal consultations regarding jointly owned properties and real estate inheritance
  • Preparation and review of construction contracts and undertakings
  • Boundary disputes between properties

Why choose Al-Arabia for real estate matters?

We have extensive experience in dealing with all complex real estate cases, providing practical legal solutions tailored to each situation, with careful follow-up of procedures until completion.

Our deep knowledge of Syria's property registration systems and real estate taxes enables us to provide precise legal advice that protects our clients from potential risks.
