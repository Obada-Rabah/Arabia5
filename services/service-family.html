Legal Support in Family Cases

At Al-Arabia , we provide comprehensive legal consultations for individuals and families in Syria, in accordance with the Syrian Personal Status Law, to ensure the protection of your rights and interests.

Our Family Law services include:

  • Marriage registration for Syrians inside and outside Syria
  • Divorce registration inside and outside Syria
  • Custody and alimony cases
  • Inheritance and estate distribution
  • Settlement of family disputes through amicable or judicial means
  • Legal consultations in guardianship and custody cases
  • Registration of births and deaths inside and outside Syria

Why choose Al-Arabia Law Firm?

We have extensive experience and precise knowledge of Syrian personal status laws, and we are committed to providing realistic and customized legal solutions for each case to ensure the best possible outcomes.

We strive to represent our clients with the highest levels of competence and professionalism, and we are keen to follow up on their cases with precision and great attention until justice is achieved for them.
